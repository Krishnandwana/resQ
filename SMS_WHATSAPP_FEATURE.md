# 📱 SMS + WhatsApp Emergency Feature

## ✅ Feature Successfully Added!

Your ResQHer app now has **BOTH SMS and WhatsApp** emergency alert features!

---

## 🚨 How It Works

### **When You Press SOS Button:**

1. **5-second countdown starts**
2. **After countdown finishes:**
   - ✅ **SMS sent automatically** to all primary contacts
   - ✅ **WhatsApp button appears**

---

## 📲 Dual Alert System

### **Option 1: SMS (Automatic)** ✅ PRIMARY

**What happens:**
- Sends immediately after countdown
- No user action needed
- Works without internet
- Recipient receives:
  ```
  EMERGENCY ALERT! I need immediate help!
  This is an automated message from ResQHer app.

  My current location:
  [Your address or coordinates]
  https://maps.google.com/?q=12.9716,77.5946
  ```

**Advantages:**
- ✅ **Completely automatic**
- ✅ **Works offline** (no internet needed)
- ✅ **Universal** (works on all phones)
- ✅ **Reliable** in emergencies

---

### **Option 2: WhatsApp (Manual)** ✅ ADDITIONAL

**What happens:**
- Green "Share on WhatsApp" button appears after SMS sent
- Tap button → WhatsApp opens
- Message is pre-filled with:
  ```
  🚨 EMERGENCY ALERT! 🚨

  I need immediate help!

  My current location:
  [Your address]
  https://maps.google.com/?q=12.9716,77.5946

  This is an automated emergency message from ResQHer safety app.
  ```
- **You must manually select contacts and press Send**

**Advantages:**
- ✅ **Visual confirmation** (emojis, formatting)
- ✅ **Free** (uses WhatsApp data)
- ✅ **Group messaging** (send to multiple groups)
- ✅ **Delivery receipts** (blue ticks)

**Disadvantages:**
- ❌ **Requires internet** (WiFi or mobile data)
- ❌ **Manual** (you must click Send)
- ❌ **Requires WhatsApp installed**

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### **SMS sent but WhatsApp button not showing?**
- Button appears only after SMS is sent
- Wait a few seconds after "Emergency alerts sent!" message

### **WhatsApp says "Not installed"?**
- Install WhatsApp from Play Store
- Or just use SMS (it already worked!)

### **Can I skip WhatsApp?**
- Yes! SMS already sent automatically
- WhatsApp is just an optional extra

### **Can I send to groups on WhatsApp?**
- Yes! When WhatsApp opens:
  - Tap the search bar
  - Select any groups
  - Tap Send
